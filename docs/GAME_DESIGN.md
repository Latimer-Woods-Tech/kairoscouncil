## Core Thesis
Card power is not arbitrary. It is calculated from real planetary positions, publicly verifiable, constantly changing.

## Game Modes
- **1v1 Transit (PvP)** — Race to Transit Clock 13
- **Eclipse (Co-op PvE)** — Team vs Shadow AI during real eclipse events
- **Chart (Auto-Battler)** — Async deck simulation with shareable narrative
