# ADR-008: Lux Distortion (Never Removal)

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-04-15
**Constant:** AC-008

## Context
Lux attacks originally hid information completely. Playtesting showed this felt unfair — players felt cheated rather than challenged (LL-004).

## Decision
Lux always distorts, never removes. TP → range (±8), Clock → range (±1), Forecast → general mood, Forge Intensity → descriptor.

## Consequences
- Players still make meaningful decisions under Lux — they just have less precision
- "Fog of war" feeling without "cheated" feeling
- Probabilistic thinking becomes a trainable skill
